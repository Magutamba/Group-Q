Good Health and Wellbeing

Who are we?

We are a collective tasked with creating sustainable solutions. For our collective our solutions are specifically targeted towards the third sustainability "Good health and well-being" goal of the global goals.

The Goal

The third goal of the global sustainability goals is to promote good health and well-being for all people across the world.

Target 3.3: Fighting communicable disease

An example of our efforts towards this is our work towards the end of epidemics such as neglected tropical disease and water-borne disease.
