Good Health and Wellbeing


Who are we?

We are a collective tasked with creating sustainable solutions. For our collective our solutions are specifically targeted towards the third sustainability "Good health and well-being" goal of the global goals.


The Goal

The third goal of the global sustainability goals is to promote good health and well-being for all peopl across the world.


Target 3.3: Fighting communicable disease

An example of our efforts towards this is our efforts to end epidemics by 2030 such as neglected tropical disease and water-borne disease.
